Assessment Context

Severe Weather Threat

Check active alerts for the selected county at forecast.weather.gov or the NWS Norman forecast office. The Storm Prediction Center (spc.noaa.gov) provides outlooks for tornado and hail threats.

Active lightning within 8 miles forces Outdoor Exposure to Red regardless of other scores.

Winter Hazards

Ice accumulation and wind chill forecasts are available from the NWS area forecast for each county. The Oklahoma Mesonet provides real-time observed temperatures, wind speeds, and precipitation data from stations across the state, including stations near each campus. Road condition observations can supplement forecast data.

Particularly dangerous in morning hours on untreated surfaces.

Heat Hazards

Heat index forecasts are available from the NWS area forecast. Wet Bulb Globe Temperature (WBGT) is a more accurate heat stress measure and is available through Perry Weather during warm months. If WBGT is entered, it will override the Heat Index in the calculation.

Available from Perry Weather. Preferred over Heat Index when available.

Wind Conditions

Sustained wind and gust forecasts are available from the NWS area forecast and Oklahoma Mesonet current observations. High Wind Warnings and Wind Advisories will appear in the NWS alert feed.

Road & Travel Conditions

Campus walkway and parking lot conditions should be confirmed with Facilities Management or University Police when available.

Based on Facilities Management or University Police ground assessment.
