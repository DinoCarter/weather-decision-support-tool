Beta Testing Notice

This Weather Decision Support Tool (WDST) is currently in beta testing and validation.

The tool is under active development and should not be used as the sole basis for any operational decisions.

The information, scores, and recommendations generated by this tool are intended to support operational decision-making but do not replace professional judgment, official weather products, university policy, emergency procedures, or direction from authorized leadership.

This tool is a decision-support aid only and does not constitute an official University directive, policy determination, or operational decision.
